? How to Use — First Time? ▾
1
Select or Create a Group
Pick your friend group from the dropdown, or tap "➕ Create New Group" for a new circle. Each group has its own leaderboard.
2
Set the Buy-in Unit
This is how much ₹ one buy-in costs (e.g. ₹500). You can change it each session.
3
Add Players & Track Buy-ins
Add everyone at the table. Use the + button each time someone re-buys in. The counter shows how many buy-ins they have.
⚠️
Don't Use +/− to Settle!
The +/− buttons are only for buy-ins during the game. Do NOT use them to track who won or lost. The app does that for you at the end!
4
End Session → Enter Cash-Out
When the game is over, tap "♦ End Session". Enter how much ₹ each player is walking away with. The app calculates who owes whom.
5
Save & Check the Leaderboard
Hit "Save & Close" to record the session. All profits, win rates, and rankings are updated on the Leaderboard page automatically.
💡 Tip: If someone books profit mid-game and leaves, just tap − to make their buy-in negative. At the end, enter ₹0 as their cash-out. The math handles it!
